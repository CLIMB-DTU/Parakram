Steps To Apply

Please go through the following steps before filling the form!!

Select The Events

  • Choose the event(s) and speakers that you want to apply for.
  • Some events have limited slots and are first come first get basis.

Fill Out The Form

  • Go to the registration form. There is a single registration form for all the events and can be accessed from any event description.
  • Fill in your details in the Google Form and for payment click on the MILAAP link given in the form.

Pay And Submit Screenshot

  • Pay the required amount throuh the MILAAP link and take a screenshot of the payment confirmation sent by MILAAP
  • Attach the screenshot in the Google form and submit the form.

 
NOTE: Do not make anonymous payments. Add a note: For 'Parakram' ( CLIMB-DTU), From YOUR NAME. We won't be able to consider your candidature if payment is anonymous.
